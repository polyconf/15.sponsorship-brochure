About Poly Conf

PolyConf is a three-day, single track, multi-disciplinary conference on advanced technologies for programmers interested in polyglot approach to software development.

History

PolyConf succeeds RuPy, a Ruby and Python conference founded in 2006. Over seven years the conference has grown and disseminated to several countries (Poland, Czech Republic, Hungary, Brazil) as well as to other programming languages.

Mission

PolyConf mission is to promote polyglot approach to software development and foster cross-pollination between various technologies and their communities.

Date & Location

June 30th - 2nd 2016

PolyConf will happen at The Faculty of Law and Administration of Adam Mickiewicz University in the heart of the city of POZnan, Poland.
